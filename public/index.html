Open Port Checker

This tool attempts to connect to common ports on your public IP address to see if they are open.

Why check for open ports?

  • Open ports can be a security risk if not managed properly.
  • Some VPNs or proxies might leave certain ports open, which can be a characteristic that distinguishes them from typical user connections.
  • This test helps you understand what services might be externally accessible on your IP.
